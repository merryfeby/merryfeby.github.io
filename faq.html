FAQ
Below are some answers to our most frequently asked questions!

Flowery offers flower delivery all across Indonesia - from the Jakarta all the way to Papua. Flower delivery costs will vary depending on the suburb for delivery. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact the Flowery florist team in Jakarta

Orders received before 10am will receive same day delivery. Orders made after 10am will be sent next day. If you place your order after 10am and still need your flowers sent same day we can do an express delivery but fees will apply (this ranges from $25-$35). Please note that due to notorious traffic delays in our city we cannot guarantee specific times of delivery for any orders

If no one is home, we will try to find a safe area, away from the sunlight, to leave the flowers. To avoid drying the flowers out, we will keep them in a temporary water source. If you know you won't be home to collect your flowers, we recommend adding a vase to your order, so your flowers will stay as fresh as possible during the wait. If we can't find a space and a shady space, we will contact the customers and arrange a redelivery when they are at the delivery address. This will sometimes incur an additional fee, so please advise our best options on the order form, with as much information as possible to avoid this.
